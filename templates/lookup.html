Lonely Person Community

This website helps you find people who believe the same things as you do.

Are you tired of not having a place to discuss things?

This is a discussion website powered by opinions. It works like this:

  1. You take a quiz
  2. Your results are analysed
  3. Based on the results of the quiz, a community is created for you or if it already exists, you are sent to it.
  4. You can create comments on your community page.
  5. You can bookmark the community page by clicking 'Link to community'.
  6. You can add your own questions by clicking Add question.
